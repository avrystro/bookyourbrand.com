## Brand Identity

Book Your Brand uses a **premium literary** aesthetic, distinct from EVB:
- **Palette:** Deep charcoal (#141821) + warm gold (#c9a55c) + cream (#f5f0e8) + navy (#1e2a3a)
- **Fonts:** Playfair Display (headings) + Inter (body)
- **Feel:** Editorial, sophisticated, high-end publishing. NOT gamified, NOT neon.

## Tech Stack
