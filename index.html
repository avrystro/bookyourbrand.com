Meet Your Author Coach

David Lloyd Strauss

Internationally Acclaimed Writer & Author Coach

David is an internationally celebrated bestselling writer, editor, and author coach with over 40 years of literary experience. He offers personalized coaching and writing services for aspiring authors who want to be the authentic voice of their book while benefiting from his decades of craft.

As a prolific writer with over a dozen books of his own, David provides expert guidance through every stage: concept development, structured outlining, manuscript coaching, developmental and line editing, proofreading, and step-by-step guidance through self-publishing.

"My superpower is taking a person's story and making it the reader's story. Your book should change the person who reads it."

With his unique "Done With You" method, David has helped dozens of authors worldwide bring their stories to life through his one-on-one, conversational coaching style. He serves as an experienced guide, editor, co-writer, and sounding board, helping you shape your ideas into a manuscript worthy of publication.

The Journey

From Idea to Published Bestseller

A proven process refined over 40 years and dozens of published authors. Every step designed to keep your authentic voice front and center.

01

Discovery Call

We start with a conversation. What's your story? Who needs to hear it? What does success look like for your book? This call determines if we're the right fit.

02

Story Architecture

Together we map out your book's structure, key themes, and narrative arc. This blueprint becomes the foundation everything builds on.

03

Conversational Writing

Through recorded conversations, I extract your stories, insights, and voice. You talk, I listen and write. Every chapter sounds like you because it comes from you.

04

Craft & Polish

I shape the raw material into compelling prose. Developmental editing, line editing, and multiple revision rounds until every sentence earns its place.

05

Your Approval

You review every chapter. Nothing goes to print without your full sign-off. This is your book, your name, your legacy. I make sure it represents you perfectly.

06

Publication & Beyond

Cover design, formatting, ISBN, Amazon listing, bestseller launch strategy. I guide you through every step from manuscript to published author.
