T H E   W O R D S M I T H

Meet David

Books gave me a second chance at life. While visiting the ancient ruins at Chaco Canyon, New Mexico, I was struck by a rockfall to the head. The recovery was long and uncertain, and it changed the course of everything. It led me to write my first two books, Footsteps After the Fall and Dancing with Energy Vampires, and it set me on a path I have followed for more than forty years.

I am an internationally celebrated bestselling writer, editor, and author coach. I offer personalized coaching and writing services for aspiring writers who want to be the authentic authors of their book while benefiting from my writing and editing skills and four decades of literary experience. With over a dozen books of my own, and dozens more for clients, I provide expert guidance throughout the entire writing and editing process: concept development, structured outlining, manuscript coaching, developmental and line editing, proofreading, and step-by-step guidance through self-publishing.

I believe that when people are struggling in life and trying to figure things out, they will go to a book first, before they go to an actual person. They read books to help them make sense of life, to find answers, to feel less alone in whatever they are facing. In my world, I help people write books for the readers who are searching for those answers. We position your book as the answer to their problem.

Mentored by Sharon Lechter, Tony Robbins, and Bob Proctor, I believe the most important decision you will ever make is the people you choose to associate with. My work and story have been featured across major media outlets, including ABC, NBC, CBS, and FOX TV networks, NPR, and Success Today TV, as well as at a private reception at the United Nations and on dozens of podcasts, live stages, and webinars worldwide.

If you have a book inside you, I would like to hear about it.

T H E   C R A F T

How a book comes to life

I

The Author Interview

Our journey begins with a comprehensive author interview: eighteen carefully developed questions that I have refined over decades of working with authors. This is a deep creative exploration designed to surface your natural voice, clarify the heart of your message, and help you think like your reader from the very start. By the time you finish, you will have greater clarity about your book than you have ever had before.

II

The Sample Manuscript

Based on your interview responses, I write a sample introduction and a complete working outline for your book, at no charge. This is your idea coming to life on the page for the very first time. You will see your story, your message, and your reader all reflected back to you in a way that is compelling, purposeful, and unmistakably yours.

III

The Book

We write together. Starting with the stories and ideas you feel most comfortable sharing, we build a full working manuscript within sixty days. Then we go chapter by chapter to refine your voice and sharpen your message, until every section serves your reader and the writing sounds like the best version of you. We play tennis with the manuscript, back and forth, until the book is done.

Every book begins with a story worth telling

Whether you are reflecting on a life well-lived, distilling years of hard-won wisdom, or giving shape to an idea that keeps you up at night, the first question is always the same: what kind of book wants to be written?

Memoir

Your life has chapters that other people need to read. A memoir captures the experiences, turning points, and quiet revelations that shaped who you are, written so that readers see their own journey reflected in yours.

Inquire →

Self-Help

You have solved a problem that others are still struggling with. A self-help book positions your insight, your method, and your lived experience as a guide for readers searching for answers they have not been able to find on their own.

Inquire →

Autobiography

The full arc of a life, told in your voice. An autobiography is a comprehensive account of where you have been, what you have built, and what you have learned along the way. It is the book people will reach for when they want to understand how you became who you are.

Inquire →

Novel

Some truths are best told through fiction. A novel lets you explore ideas, emotions, and human experiences through characters and narrative, giving your message a form that readers carry with them long after the last page.

Inquire →

Journal / Workbook

A book that works alongside the reader. Journals and workbooks combine your expertise with structured exercises, prompts, and reflection spaces, giving readers a tool they return to again and again as they apply your ideas to their own life.
